# antiChud - coming soon.

A Firefox and Chrome extension designed to keep me focused on locking in before chudmaxxing. The goal is simple: log at least three or more hours of verified study or assignment time each day before I can unlock the rest of the internet.

## Why?

- **C**hronic procrastination and slipping study habits
- **H**abitually opening Gemini for help when stumped on questions
- **U**nfocused scrolling and browsing when trying to “study”
- **D**rowning in YouTube brainrot more than learning from it

## How it should work (concept)

1) **Verification first**: Track three or more hours of study/assignment time with some form of proof (timer + manual check, screenshots, friend verification, or quiz confirmations).
2) **Lockdown by default**: Non-learning sites stay blocked until the daily study requirement is met.
3) **YouTube safeguard**: Allow only whitelisted educational content via a curated/embedded YouTube player; block the rest of the site to avoid the recommendation rabbit hole.
4) **Daily reset**: Requirements reset every day to encourage consistency.

## Initial feature checklist
